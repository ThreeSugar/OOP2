Healthy Food A


Makes 4 to 6 servings

5 or 6 large eggs (or egg substitute equivalent)

2 tablespoons to 1/3 cup milk, half-and-half, cream or dairy substitute

Salt, pepper and a dash of nutmeg or cayenne, or favorite seasoning mix, and maybe a dash of hot sauce

1 frozen 9-inch deep-dish pie shell, or refrigerated pie crust (optional)

2 to 2-1/2 cups total: Any type shredded cheese

Chopped, well-drained cooked or raw vegetables

Diced deli meat or other cooked diced meat, seafood or poultry

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat eggs and milk in a large mixing bowl.
